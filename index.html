Let's Get Started!

The Global GoGreen Challenge is a movement that inspires people to make small changes in their lifestyle, in order to generate huge changes in the world.


The challenge involves individuals undertaking the task of adopting a vegan or vegetarian diet for a certain period of time. With the GoGreen app, you can keep track of your progress, get useful advice and reminders, and visualize your impact on our planet, your power to make things better. Even more, you can invite your friends to join the challenge, see their progress, and share photos of your experience with them!

So, what are you waiting for? Download the app and challenge your friends!
